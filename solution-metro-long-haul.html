Metro / Transport Networks

Long-reach and transport optical solutions for metro and long-haul—with focus on interoperability, platform compatibility, supply continuity, and channel support for operators and integrators.

Overview

ASTERON supplies long-reach and BiDi optical modules for metro and long-haul transport through qualified partners. Buyers get application-appropriate reach and form factors, supply continuity, delivery flexibility, and North America channel support; qualification status varies by platform and partner.

Typical Applications

  • Metro and regional transport
  • Long-haul and backbone links
  • Single-fiber BiDi and WDM deployments

Product families used in this solution: 800G Pluggables, 400G Pluggables, Coherent / ZR / ZR+. See Products for the full catalog.

Key Considerations

  • Interoperability — Compatibility with transport and line systems; qualification status varies.
  • Supply continuity — Lead times and volume from qualified partners.
  • Platform compatibility — Confirm host and management support for your deployment.
  • Delivery flexibility — Project-based and volume options.
  • Channel support — North America–oriented support for operators and integrators.
