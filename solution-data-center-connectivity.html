Cloud & DCI

Coherent pluggable options for DCI—with focus on interoperability, platform compatibility, supply continuity, and delivery flexibility. Qualification status and availability vary by product and partner.

Overview

ASTERON offers access to coherent and ZR/ZR+ optical modules for data center interconnect through qualified partners. Value for buyers includes interoperability with common host platforms, supply continuity, and channel support in North America. Selected products (e.g. 800G ZR+, extended reach) may be roadmap or available upon request.

Typical Applications

  • Data center interconnects (DCI) and metro links
  • Cloud and hyperscale buildouts
  • Point-to-point and ring topologies

Product families used in this solution: 800G Pluggables, 400G Pluggables, Coherent / ZR / ZR+. See Products for the full catalog.

Key Considerations

  • Interoperability — Compatibility with target switch and line systems; qualification status varies by platform.
  • Supply continuity — Lead times and volume from qualified manufacturing partners.
  • Platform compatibility — Confirm host and management support (e.g. CMIS) for your deployment.
  • Delivery flexibility — Project-based and volume sourcing; some variants available upon request.
  • Channel support — North America–oriented commercial and technical support.
