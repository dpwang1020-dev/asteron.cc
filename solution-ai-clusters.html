AI Infrastructure

Optical connectivity for AI clusters and hyperscale data centers—with focus on interoperability, supply continuity, thermal performance, and North America–oriented channel support.

Overview

ASTERON supplies qualified optical modules for AI and data center applications through a partner-based supply chain. Buyers get platform compatibility, delivery flexibility, and channel support; product availability and qualification status vary by platform and partner.

Typical Applications

  • AI training and inference clusters
  • Hyperscale data center spine and leaf
  • GPU-to-GPU and GPU-to-switch connectivity
  • High-bandwidth intra- and inter-rack links

Product families used in this solution: 1.6T Roadmap, 800G Pluggables, 400G Pluggables, AOC / DAC. See Products for the full catalog.

Key Considerations

  • Interoperability — Compatibility with major switch and host platforms; qualification status varies.
  • Supply continuity — Lead times and volume flexibility through qualified partners.
  • Thermal performance — Suitability for dense rack environments; specs depend on product and platform.
  • Platform compatibility — Confirm support for your host and optics management (CMIS, etc.).
  • Delivery flexibility — Project-based and volume options; availability upon request for select families.
  • Channel support — North America–facing sales and technical support.
