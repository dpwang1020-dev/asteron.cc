About ASTERON

A leading global manufacturer of active optical communication devices and modules.

ASTERON is a leading global manufacturer of active optical communication devices and modules. With extensive expertise in datacom/telecom, manufacturing, and robust R&D capabilities, we drive innovation and deliver exceptional value to our customers. Our solutions are specifically designed for AI clouds and data centers.

Our Focus

We provide high-speed pluggable transceivers and optical interconnect solutions for AI clusters, data center connectivity, storage and HPC, wireless fronthaul, metro and long-haul transport, and fiber-to-the-home access—enabling the next generation of networks worldwide.
