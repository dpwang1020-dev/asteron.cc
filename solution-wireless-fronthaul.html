Mobile & Fronthaul

Optical modules for mobile fronthaul and carrier networks—with focus on interoperability, supply continuity, platform compatibility, and channel support for operators and integrators.

Overview

ASTERON provides access to optical modules for 4G, 5G, and 5G-A fronthaul and mobile backhaul through qualified partners. Value for buyers includes application fit (gray, BiDi, colored), delivery flexibility, and North America–oriented channel support; availability and qualification vary by product and partner.

Typical Applications

  • Mobile fronthaul (AAU–DU, DU–BBU)
  • 5G and 5G-A radio and small cell connectivity
  • Carrier access and aggregation

Product families used in this solution: Access Optics (Mobile / Fronthaul). See Products for the full catalog.

Key Considerations

  • Interoperability — Compatibility with DU/BBU and OLT platforms; qualification status varies.
  • Supply continuity — Lead times and volume from qualified partners.
  • Platform compatibility — Confirm support for your radio and transport equipment.
  • Delivery flexibility — Project-based and volume options.
  • Channel support — North America–oriented support for operators and integrators.

Product availability and qualification status may vary by platform, application, and partner manufacturer.
